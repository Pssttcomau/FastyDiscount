## Full Description (App Store — up to 4000 characters)

FastyDiscount is the ultimate digital wallet for all your discount vouchers, gift cards, and promotional codes.

**Never Let a Discount Expire Again**
Store every paper coupon, email voucher, and gift card digitally. FastyDiscount sends smart push notifications before your discounts expire, so you always redeem in time.

**Instant Barcode & QR Scan**
Point your camera at any barcode or QR code on a voucher and FastyDiscount imports the details instantly. No typing required.

**Location-Aware Alerts**
Walk past a store where one of your vouchers can be redeemed and FastyDiscount taps you on the shoulder — even when the app is closed. Our geofencing technology monitors up to 20 stores simultaneously in the background.

**Everything in One Place**
- Barcodes & QR codes (scan or import from photos/PDF)
- Gift card values and balances
- Promotional and discount codes
- Expiry dates with automatic reminders
- Full-screen barcode display for easy scanning at the till

**Map & Store Discovery**
See all your redeemable vouchers on a live map. Filter by category or store to find what's nearby.

**Apple Watch Support**
Your most-used vouchers sync automatically to Apple Watch. Scan barcodes straight from your wrist.

**Widgets & Lock Screen**
Glanceable widgets show your soonest-expiring or most-recently used vouchers on your Home Screen or Lock Screen.

**iCloud Sync**
Your vouchers sync securely across all your Apple devices via iCloud. No account required beyond Sign in with Apple.

**Privacy First**
FastyDiscount uses Sign in with Apple so you never hand over your real email address. Location data is processed on-device and never uploaded to our servers.

**Remove Ads**
Enjoying the app? Remove all ads with a single one-time purchase.
