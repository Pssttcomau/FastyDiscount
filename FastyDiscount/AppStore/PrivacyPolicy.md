## 1. Introduction

FastyDiscount ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This
Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used
when you use the FastyDiscount app ("the App").

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Location Data (Precise)

**What**: Your precise GPS location.
**Why**: To trigger alerts when you are near a store where one of your saved vouchers
can be redeemed (geofencing).
**How stored**: Location is processed entirely on-device using Apple's Core Location
framework. Your precise location is **never** transmitted to our servers or shared
with third parties.
**Your control**: You can disable location access at any time in iOS Settings >
Privacy & Security > Location Services > FastyDiscount.

### 2.2 Email Address and Email Content (Optional)

**What**: Your Gmail email address and the content of promotional emails in your inbox.
**Why**: To automatically import voucher codes and discount details from promotional
emails, with your explicit consent via Google OAuth.
**How stored**: Email content is processed on-device to extract voucher data. The raw
content of your emails is **never** transmitted to our servers.
**Your control**: You can revoke Gmail access at any time in Google Account >
Security > Third-party apps with account access.

### 2.3 Purchase History

**What**: Whether you have purchased the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase.
**Why**: To grant you the ad-free experience you purchased.
**How stored**: Purchase entitlements are managed entirely by Apple's StoreKit 2
framework. We do not store any payment details.

### 2.4 Advertising Identifiers (IDFA)

**What**: Your device's advertising identifier (IDFA), collected by Google AdMob.
**Why**: To serve personalised advertisements in the free version of the App.
**Your control**: You will be asked for your permission via the App Tracking Transparency
(ATT) prompt when you first open the App. You can change your choice at any time in
iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.
If you decline, only contextual (non-personalised) ads will be shown.

### 2.5 Sign in with Apple Identifier

**What**: Your stable Sign in with Apple user identifier.
**Why**: To authenticate you and link your iCloud sync data.
**How stored**: This identifier is used solely to identify your iCloud CloudKit
container. It is never shared with third parties.
